## 2. Critical Metrics Amazon Sellers Need

### 2.1 Top 10 Most Important KPIs

Based on 2025 seller surveys and tool analysis, here are the essential metrics:

#### 1. **Net Profit & Net Profit Margin** (PRIORITY #1)
- **Formula:** `(Total Revenue - All Expenses) / Total Revenue √ó 100`
- **Target:** 15-20% minimum, 20-30% for successful sellers
- **Why Critical:** Only metric that shows true business health
- **Calculation Requirements:**
  - Revenue from all sources
  - Amazon referral fees (8-45%, avg 15%)
  - FBA fulfillment fees
  - Storage fees (standard & oversize)
  - PPC costs
  - COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
  - Returns & refunds (including damaged goods)
  - 70+ "hidden" Amazon fees
  - Shipping costs (inbound & outbound)

#### 2. **Gross Profit**
- **Formula:** `Total Revenue - COGS - Amazon Fees`
- **Why Important:** Shows profitability before operating expenses
- **Use Case:** Quick product viability assessment

#### 3. **Sales Revenue & Sales Velocity**
- **Total Sales:** Revenue from all product sales over period
- **Sales Velocity:** `Units Sold / Average Inventory Level`
- **Why Critical:**
  - Measures inventory efficiency
  - Impacts IPI score (Inventory Performance Index)
  - Affects storage fees and capacity limits

#### 4. **ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)**
- **Formula:** `Ad Spend / Ad Revenue √ó 100`
- **Target:** Below break-even ACOS for profitability
- **Why Critical:** Measures PPC campaign efficiency
- **Insight:** Lower ACOS = more efficient advertising

#### 5. **TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales)**
- **Formula:** `Ad Spend / Total Revenue (Organic + Paid) √ó 100`
- **Why Important:** Shows true advertising impact on business
- **Trend:** Decreasing TACOS = growing organic sales, building brand
- **Use Case:** Long-term growth measurement vs. ACOS for campaigns

#### 6. **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**
- **Formula:** `Ad Sales / Ad Spend`
- **Average:** 3x on Amazon
- **Why Critical:** Inverse of ACOS - easier to understand ROI
- **Example:** ROAS of 4 = $4 revenue for every $1 spent

#### 7. **Conversion Rate**
- **Formula:** `Orders / Sessions √ó 100`
- **Why Critical:** "Arguably the most important KPI" per experts
- **Impact:** Shows listing quality, pricing effectiveness, customer confidence
- **Benchmark:** Amazon average 10-15%

#### 8. **IPI Score (Inventory Performance Index)**
- **Range:** 0-1000
- **Target:** 450+ to avoid restrictions
- **Components:**
  - Excess inventory percentage
  - FBA sell-through rate
  - Stranded inventory percentage
  - In-stock rate
- **Why Critical:** Determines FBA capacity limits
- **Impact:** Low IPI = storage restrictions, higher fees

#### 9. **BSR (Best Seller Rank)**
- **What It Is:** Product's sales rank vs. similar products in category
- **Updates:** Hourly
- **Why Critical:**
  - Sales performance indicator (lower = better)
  - Competitive benchmarking
  - Early warning for issues
  - Product research for new opportunities
- **Problem:** Amazon doesn't provide historical data
- **Solution:** Third-party tools must track BSR history

#### 10. **ODR (Order Defect Rate)**
- **Formula:** `(Negative feedback + A-to-Z claims + Chargebacks) / Total Orders √ó 100`
- **Target:** Under 1%
- **Why Critical:** Affects account health and Buy Box eligibility
- **Components:**
  - Negative feedback
  - A-to-Z Guarantee claims
  - Chargebacks

### 2.4 Profit Calculation Deep Dive

#### Amazon FBA Fee Structure (2025)

**Referral Fees:**
- 8-45% by category
- Most common: 15%
- No increases announced for 2025

**FBA Fulfillment Fees:**
- Based on product size/weight tier
- Small standard: ~$3.22
- Large standard: ~$5.32-$8.73
- Oversize: $10.05+

**Storage Fees (per cubic foot/month):**
- Standard (Jan-Sep): $0.78
- Standard (Oct-Dec): $2.40
- Oversize (Jan-Sep): $0.53
- Oversize (Oct-Dec): $1.40
- **Improvement:** Down from $0.87 to $0.78 off-peak (2025)

**Overage Fees:**
- $10 per cubic foot/month beyond capacity limits

**Other Fees to Account For:**
- Long-term storage fees (181+ days)
- Removal/disposal fees
- Return processing fees
- Inbound placement fees (with reductions)
- Aged inventory surcharges
- Multi-channel fulfillment fees

#### Accurate Profit Formula

```
Net Profit = Revenue
  - COGS
  - Referral Fees
  - FBA Fulfillment Fees
  - Storage Fees
  - PPC Costs
  - Returns (product value + fees)
  - Refunds
  - Promotional Discounts
  - Inbound Shipping
  - Amazon Reimbursements (add back)
```

#### Break-Even ACOS Calculation

```
Break-Even ACOS = (Selling Price - Total Costs) / Selling Price √ó 100

Example:
Selling Price: $30
COGS: $8
Amazon Fees: $10
Total Cost: $18
Margin: $12

Break-Even ACOS = $12 / $30 √ó 100 = 40%
```

**Critical Insight:** Must calculate per product as ACOS targets vary wildly

## 3. Amazon Seller Pain Points

### 3.1 Financial Challenges

**Cash Flow Crisis:**
- Payment cycle: 14 days + 3-5 days bank transfer + 7-day reserve = 20+ days to receive funds
- Reality: 3-4 months from purchase to payment when including manufacturing, shipping, customs
- 60% of small businesses experience cash flow problems
- Solution demand: Express Payouts (24 hours for fee), cash flow forecasting tools

**Fee Confusion:**
- Sellers losing money due to improper fee calculations
- 70+ fee types difficult to track manually
- FBA calculator often not used (costly mistake)
- Demand: Automatic fee calculation with transparency

**Hidden Profit Killers:**
- Returns costing product value + fees
- Damaged goods not reimbursed
- Lost inventory in FBA network
- Storage fees accumulating unnoticed

## 4. Dashboard Design Best Practices

### 4.1 Layout & Organization Principles

**Screen Real Estate:**
- Limit to 5-6 cards in initial view
- Single-screen dashboard improves UX (avoid scrolling for critical data)
- Human brain processes ~7-9 items effectively - don't overwhelm

**Visual Hierarchy:**
- Most important metrics at top-left (Western reading pattern)
- Use size to indicate importance
- Progressive disclosure: overview ‚Üí drill-down details

**Component Types:**
1. **Tiles/Cards:** Summary metrics with change indicators
2. **Charts:** Trend analysis and relationships
3. **Tables:** Product lists with sortable columns
4. **Maps:** Regional performance visualization
5. **Alerts:** Actionable notifications requiring attention

---

### 4.2 Data Visualization Best Practices

**Chart Selection:**
- **Line Charts:** Time-series data (sales trends, profit over time)
- **Bar Charts:** Category comparisons (product performance, marketplace comparison)
- **Pie/Donut Charts:** Composition (expense breakdown, sales by category) - use sparingly
- **Area Charts:** Cumulative metrics (total revenue growth)
- **Heatmaps:** Multi-dimensional data (BSR tracking across products/time)

**Color Psychology:**
- **Green:** Positive metrics, profit, growth, success indicators
- **Red:** Negative metrics, losses, alerts, declining performance
- **Blue:** Neutral information, primary actions, trust
- **Amber/Yellow:** Warnings, attention needed, moderate priority
- **Gray:** Inactive, disabled, secondary information

**Design Standards:**
- Consistent color usage throughout dashboard
- Icons with hover animations for interactivity
- Cognitive landmarks: Show averages, targets, benchmarks for context
- Data-first approach: Visuals enhance understanding, not decorate

---

### 4.3 Interactive Elements

**Essential Interactions:**
- **Time Range Selector:** Quick toggles (Today, Yesterday, 7D, 30D, Custom)
- **Click to Filter:** Drill down from overview to product details
- **Sortable Tables:** Click column headers to sort
- **Expandable Rows:** Show/hide detailed product information
- **Hover States:** Tooltips with additional context
- **Comparison Mode:** Period-over-period analysis

**Filter Capabilities:**
- Product/SKU search
- Category filtering
- Marketplace selection (multi-marketplace sellers)
- Date range customization
- Status filters (Active, Inactive, Out of Stock)

## 9. Specific Metrics Calculation Formulas

### 9.1 Profit Metrics

```javascript
// Net Profit
netProfit = totalRevenue
  - cogs
  - referralFees
  - fbaFees
  - storageFees
  - adSpend
  - returnsValue
  - refundsValue
  + reimbursements

// Net Profit Margin
netMargin = (netProfit / totalRevenue) * 100

// Gross Profit
grossProfit = totalRevenue - cogs - referralFees - fbaFees

// Gross Margin
grossMargin = (grossProfit / totalRevenue) * 100

// ROI
roi = (netProfit / totalCosts) * 100
```

---

### 9.2 PPC Metrics

```javascript
// ACOS
acos = (adSpend / adRevenue) * 100

// TACOS
tacos = (adSpend / totalRevenue) * 100

// ROAS
roas = adRevenue / adSpend

// Break-Even ACOS
breakEvenAcos = ((sellingPrice - allCosts) / sellingPrice) * 100

// CTR
ctr = (clicks / impressions) * 100

// CPC
cpc = adSpend / clicks

// Conversion Rate
conversionRate = (orders / clicks) * 100
```

---

### 9.3 Inventory Metrics

```javascript
// Days of Supply
daysOfSupply = currentStock / averageDailySales

// Reorder Point
reorderPoint = (avgDailySales * leadTimeDays) + safetyStock

// Reorder Quantity
reorderQuantity = reorderPoint - currentStock - incomingStock

// Inventory Turnover Rate
turnoverRate = costOfGoodsSold / averageInventoryValue

// Sell-Through Rate
sellThroughRate = (unitsSold / (unitsReceived + startingInventory)) * 100

// IPI Score (Amazon's formula - simplified estimate)
ipiScore = (sellThroughRate * 0.4)
  + (inStockRate * 0.3)
  + ((1 - excessInventoryRate) * 0.2)
  + ((1 - strandedInventoryRate) * 0.1)
```

---

### 9.4 Account Health Metrics

```javascript
// ODR (Order Defect Rate)
odr = ((negativeFeedback + aToZClaims + chargebacks) / totalOrders) * 100

// Late Shipment Rate
lateShipmentRate = (lateShipments / totalShipments) * 100

// Pre-Fulfillment Cancel Rate
cancelRate = (canceledOrders / totalOrders) * 100

// Valid Tracking Rate
trackingRate = (ordersWithTracking / totalOrders) * 100
```
